## Must be confirmed before launch

- Confirm that the phone and email above are the preferred lead destinations for this domain.
- Confirm current campaign prices against the current PODA offer and the owner's commercial authorization.
- Add the privacy-policy controller: name or company, address, IČO if applicable, and a privacy contact.
- Confirm the correct relation to PODA a.s. Keep the representative disclaimer unless the corporate owner explicitly approves different wording.
- The first static Vercel version prepares a lead e-mail in the visitor's local mail application. Test it after deployment.
- Before replacing the local mail handoff, confirm the approved server-side form processor and update the privacy policy.
- Add GA4 only after a measurement ID is supplied and consent requirements are decided.

## SEO guardrails

- Do not copy text from competitor sites.
- Do not create pages solely to funnel visitors to the same destination.
- Do not add unsupported superlatives such as "nejlepší", "nejrychlejší v Ostravě", or "garantovaná dostupnost".
- Do not add fake testimonials, star ratings, LocalBusiness addresses, or review schema.
- Use a focused number of local pages with useful district-specific copy.

## Visual direction

- Build the visual identity around modern industrial Ostrava: steel, brick, dark surfaces, cobalt blue, and restrained safety yellow.
- Use the generated `assets/hero-ostrava-industrial.webp` visual as the homepage hero asset.
- Keep animations subtle and useful: reveal content on scroll, animate network accents, and preserve `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Do not copy a competitor's layout, image composition, text, or unsupported claims.
